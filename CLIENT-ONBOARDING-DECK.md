# Destiny Springs CRM Client Onboarding Deck

## Slide 1 - Welcome to Destiny Springs CRM
- Built for behavioral health admissions, referral growth, and relationship management
- Designed for Destiny Springs teams, referring facilities, and liaison workflows
- Powered by Aegis AI for guidance, drafting, prioritization, and safe action suggestions

Speaker notes:
This platform is not just a database. It is a working command center for behavioral health business development, admissions coordination, and referral relationship management.

## Slide 2 - What Problems We Solve
- Lost follow-ups and stalled referrals
- Fragmented communication across reps, intake, and partner facilities
- Limited visibility into who is sending, who has gone quiet, and where the pipeline is stuck
- Too much manual work for outreach, follow-up, reporting, and relationship maintenance

## Slide 3 - Core Workflows
- Track leads before they become active referral relationships
- Manage referral sources and sending facilities by territory, type, and relationship health
- Move opportunities through inquiry, clinical review, insurance auth, admitted, active, and discharged
- Log activities, site visits, lunches, in-services, and follow-ups in one place

## Slide 4 - What Aegis AI Does
- Summarizes pipeline risk and recommends next-best actions
- Drafts outreach messages, follow-up notes, and strategy plans
- Suggests safe CRM actions for human review before anything changes
- Adapts responses using role, page context, and live workload

## Slide 5 - How To Use Aegis Well
- Ask specific workflow questions: stalled admission, low census, outreach plan, reactivation strategy
- Use page context to your advantage by opening leads, opportunities, referrals, or dashboard before asking
- Review every proposed AI action before applying it
- Rate responses as useful or not useful so Aegis gets better at prioritizing what matters

## Slide 6 - Admin Experience
- Command center for active reps, open leads, opportunities, invoices, and compliance
- Audit log visibility for Aegis-originated actions
- Source filtering for AI activity and usage momentum tracking
- Central oversight for approvals, settings, and organization-wide performance

## Slide 7 - Rep Experience
- Territory-aware guidance for outreach and census recovery
- Follow-up visibility for leads and opportunities due in the next 7 days
- Quick AI prompts for quiet facilities, court strategy, detox pitch, and low census plans
- Better mobile usability for field work

## Slide 8 - Account Experience
- Clear view of active engagements, invoices, contracts, and recent activities
- Facility profile and account type management
- AI guidance framed for hospital partners and sending facilities

## Slide 9 - Safe AI Guardrails
- All AI write actions require explicit human review
- Delete actions require confirmation
- AI-originated actions are logged in the audit trail
- Validation checks prevent unsupported statuses, missing references, and invalid record shapes

## Slide 10 - Recommended Automation Menu
- Follow-up reminders for overdue leads and opportunities
- Relationship health alerts when facilities go quiet for 30+ days
- Low census action plans triggered by pipeline softness
- Scheduled referral-source reactivation campaigns
- Drafted outreach sequences for EDs, crisis units, courts, and outpatient programs
- Digest emails for reps, admins, and facility partners

## Slide 11 - Best Practices For New Clients
- Keep assigned reps, service lines, and hospital types current
- Log meaningful activities consistently so Aegis can reason from reality
- Use AI for prioritization, drafting, and planning first
- Turn on automations gradually, starting with reminders and digests before outbound campaigns

## Slide 12 - 30-Day Rollout Plan
- Week 1: configure users, territories, facilities, and service lines
- Week 2: standardize activity logging and opportunity stage discipline
- Week 3: activate Aegis usage, feedback collection, and follow-up reminders
- Week 4: enable selected automations and review audit outcomes with leadership

## Slide 13 - Executive Value Story
- Faster follow-up on referrals and at-risk relationships
- Better territory coverage and higher outreach consistency
- Cleaner reporting across referral sources, admissions pipeline, and rep activity
- AI assistance that is auditable, constrained, and operationally useful

## Slide 14 - Closing / Call to Action
- Start with the dashboard, leads, opportunities, and Aegis quick actions
- Use audit visibility and feedback to build trust early
- Expand into automations once the team has clean habits and clear ownership

Speaker notes:
The goal is not to automate everything on day one. The goal is to make the team sharper, faster, and more consistent, then let Aegis scale what already works.
